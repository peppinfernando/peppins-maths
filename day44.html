Q1 (a)4+(6−2)²×3÷8. (b)HCF and LCM of 45 and 60. (c)3/4 of 120 students passed. % failed? 8 marks
💡 (a)BEMDAS: brackets first. (b)Prime factorise both. (c)1/4 failed.
✅ Answer: (a)10. (b)HCF=15,LCM=180. (c)25% failed
Q2 Solve: (a)2x+5=11. (b)3(x−2)=2x+4. (c)x²−x−6=0. 8 marks
💡 (c)Factorise: find two numbers with product=−6 and sum=−1.
✅ Answer: (a)x=3. (b)x=10. (c)x=3 or x=−2
Q3 Sequence 5,12,19,26,… (a)Tₙ and T₁₅. (b)Which term=180? (c)S₁₀. 8 marks
💡 (b)Set 7n−2=180 and solve for n.
✅ Answer: (a)Tₙ=7n−2; T₁₅=103. (b)26th term. (c)365
Q4 Triangle: two angles 58° and y°, exterior angle 130°. (a)Find y. (b)Verify with exterior angle theorem. (c)Triangle type. 8 marks
💡 (a)Third interior angle=180−130=50°. So y=180−58−50=72°.
✅ Answer: (a)y=72°. (b)58+72=130° ✓. (c)Scalene
Q5 A(1,2), B(7,10). (a)Slope. (b)|AB|. (c)Equation of perp bisector of AB. 8 marks
💡 Perp bisector: through midpoint (4,6) with perpendicular slope −3/4.
✅ Answer: (a)4/3. (b)10. (c)3x+4y=36
Q6 Cliff: 200m from boat, angle of elevation 35°. (a)Height. (b)Lighthouse 350m from base — angle of elevation? 8 marks
💡 (a)tanθ=opposite/adjacent=h/200. (b)Now h is opposite, 350 is adjacent.
✅ Answer: (a)200tan35°≈140m. (b)tanα=140/350≈0.4 → α≈21.8°
Q7 (a)Sector area: r=8cm, θ=75°. (b)V of cylinder r=8cm, h=15cm. (c)Total SA of cylinder. 8 marks
💡 (c)Total SA=2πr²+2πrh.
✅ Answer: (a)≈41.9 cm². (b)≈3016 cm³. (c)≈1156 cm²
Q8 Bag: 5R,8B. Two without replacement. (a)P(1st red). (b)P(both red). (c)P(at least one blue). 8 marks
💡 (c)P(at least one blue)=1−P(both red)=1−5/39.
✅ Answer: (a)5/13. (b)5/39. (c)34/39
Q9 Ages: 22,30,18,25,21,15,22,27,24,20. Find mean, median, mode, range, Q1, Q3, IQR. 8 marks
💡 Order the data first: 15,18,20,21,22,22,24,25,27,30.
✅ Answer: Mean=22.4; Median=22; Mode=22; Range=15; Q1=20; Q3=25; IQR=5
Q10 Triangle: AC=7, BC=5, ∠ACB=60°. (i)Find AB. (ii)Area. (iii)Find ∠BAC (Sine Rule). 8 marks
💡 (i)Cosine Rule. (ii)½ab sinC. (iii)sin(BAC)/5=sin60/6.24.
✅ Answer: (i)AB=√39≈6.24cm. (ii)≈15.16 cm². (iii)≈43.8°
