📘 What is this topic?

Rapid-fire revision of Geometry and Trigonometry (Days 18−28). These topics account for ~30% of SEC marks — high value!

For proofs: NAME the theorem. For trig: DRAW A DIAGRAM. For coordinate geometry: SHOW the formula before substituting.

💡 Analogy

Checking all tools in your toolbox before a big job. Each question tests a different tool — confirm every one is ready.

🔍 Worked Example
Quick formula check
  1. Distance: |AB|=√[(x₂−x₁)²+(y₂−y₁)²]
  2. Line: y−y₁=m(x−x₁)
  3. Circle: (x−h)²+(y−k)²=r²
  4. SOH CAH TOA | Sine Rule | Cosine Rule — all in Tables booklet
  5. Area: ½bh; ½ab sinC; (θ/360)πr²
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • Sine vs Cosine Rule: angle BETWEEN two sides → Cosine Rule
  • Sector perimeter=arc+2 radii (not just arc!)
  • Perp slope: flip AND change sign. 3/4 → −4/3

Q1 Triangle angles: 62°, 74°, x°. Find x. 2 marks
Your Answer
💡 Angles in a triangle sum to 180°.
✅ Model Answer: x=180−62−74=44°
Q2 Cylinder half full: r=4cm, h=9cm. Volume of liquid. 2 marks
Your Answer
💡 Half the volume of a full cylinder.
✅ Model Answer: ½×π×16×9=72π≈226.2 cm³
Q3 Distance between P(2,5) and Q(5,9). 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 Apply distance formula.
✅ Model Answer: |PQ|=√[9+16]=5 units
Q4 Slope and midpoint of PQ from Q3. 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 m=(y₂−y₁)/(x₂−x₁); midpoint=average of coordinates.
✅ Model Answer: m=4/3; midpoint=(3.5,7)
Q5 Right triangle: legs 8m and 15m. Angle opposite the 15m leg. 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 Which trig ratio uses opposite (15m) and adjacent (8m)?
✅ Model Answer: tanθ=15/8 → θ=tan⁻¹(1.875)≈61.9°
Q6 Sector: r=6cm, θ=72°. Find area. 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Area=(θ/360)×πr².
✅ Model Answer: (72/360)×π×36=7.2π≈22.6 cm²
Q7 Triangle: two sides 8cm and 6cm, included angle 50°. Find third side. 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 SAS → Cosine Rule.
✅ Model Answer: c²=64+36−96cos50≈38.3; c≈6.19cm
Q8 Line through P(2,5) with slope 4/3. Equation as ax+by+c=0. 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Use y−y₁=m(x−x₁), then rearrange.
✅ Model Answer: y−5=4/3(x−2) → 4x−3y+7=0
Q9 Circle (x−3)²+(y+2)²=16. State centre and radius. 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Circle (x−h)²+(y−k)²=r². Identify h, k and r.
✅ Model Answer: Centre (3,−2), r=4
Q10 Area of triangle with vertices (2,2),(5,4),(3,1). 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Use the coordinate area formula.
✅ Model Answer: Area=½|2(4−1)+5(1−2)+3(2−4)|=½×5=2.5 sq units
