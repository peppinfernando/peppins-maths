Q1 Volume of cone: radius 3cm, height 4cm. 3 marks
💡 V=⅓πr²h. State this formula first!
✅ Answer: V=⅓π(9)(4)=12π≈37.7 cm³
Q2 Sequence 2,7,12,17,… Find Tₙ and T₈. 3 marks
💡 a=2, d=5. Use Tₙ=a+(n−1)d.
✅ Answer: Tₙ=5n−3; T₈=37
Q3 Solve: 2x²+3x−2=0 3 marks
💡 Find two numbers to factorise. Or use the quadratic formula.
✅ Answer: (2x−1)(x+2)=0; x=1/2 or x=−2
Q4 Two fair dice. P(sum=7). 3 marks
💡 List all pairs that give a sum of 7. Total outcomes=36.
✅ Answer: Pairs: (1,6)(2,5)(3,4)(4,3)(5,2)(6,1)=6/36=1/6
Q5 10m ladder, angle 52° with ground. How high does it reach? 4 marks
💡 SOH: sin(angle)=Opposite÷Hypotenuse.
✅ Answer: sin52°=h/10; h≈7.88m
Q6 Solve: 3x+4y=25 and x−2y=1 4 marks
💡 Substitution: rearrange x−2y=1 to get x=…
✅ Answer: x=2y+1; 6y+3+4y=25; y=2.2; x=5.4
Q7 Find slope and intercepts of 3x+4y=12. 4 marks
💡 x-intercept: set y=0. y-intercept: set x=0. Slope from y=mx+c form.
✅ Answer: Slope=−3/4; x-int=(4,0); y-int=(0,3)
Q8 Data: 3,1,4,2,3,5,2,1,3,4. Find mean, median, mode and IQR. 5 marks
💡 Order data first: 1,1,2,2,3,3,3,4,4,5.
✅ Answer: Mean=2.8; Median=3; Mode=3; Q1=2,Q3=4,IQR=2
Q9 Bag: 6R,9B. 3 drawn without replacement. P(exactly 2R, 1B). 5 marks
💡 Use combinations: C(6,2)×C(9,1)÷C(15,3).
✅ Answer: ⁶C₂×⁹C₁/¹⁵C₃=15×9/455=27/91
Q10 Triangle sides 9cm, 8cm, 10cm. Find the largest angle. 5 marks
💡 Largest angle is opposite longest side (10cm). Use Cosine Rule.
✅ Answer: cosA=(64+81−100)/(2×8×9)=45/144≈0.3125; A≈71.8°
