Geometry

Constructions
Ordinary 6 questions · 23 marks total
📐
📘 What is this topic?

Key constructions: (1) Perpendicular bisector of a segment. (2) Angle bisector. (3) SSS triangle. (4) SAS triangle. (5) Circumcircle (intersection of perp. bisectors). (6) Incircle (intersection of angle bisectors).

NEVER erase construction arcs in the exam — they prove your method.

💡 Analogy

The perpendicular bisector finds all points EQUIDISTANT from both endpoints. Compass arcs measure equal distances from each end — their crossings are the equidistant points.

🔍 Worked Example
Construct perpendicular bisector of AB=8cm
  1. Draw AB=8cm
  2. Set compass to more than 4cm
  3. Arcs from A above and below AB
  4. SAME compass setting: arcs from B crossing the first arcs
  5. Connect the two intersection points
  6. This line is the perpendicular bisector
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • Always use compass — not a ruler to mark the midpoint alone
  • Keep compass width THE SAME for both arcs
  • NEVER erase arcs — they show your method
📌 Key Formulae
Circumcentre: intersection of perp. bisectors of sidesIncentre: intersection of angle bisectorsSSS: compass arcs from both endsSAS: protractor for angle, compass for side lengths

Q1 Describe step-by-step how to construct the perpendicular bisector of AB=8cm. 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 You need your compass to draw arcs of equal radius from both endpoints.
✅ Model Answer: 1.Draw AB=8cm. 2.Compass>4cm, arcs from A. 3.Same setting, arcs from B. 4.Connect intersections.
Q2 Describe how to bisect a 60° angle using compass and ruler only. 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 The bisector of an angle is found by creating two equal-radius arcs.
✅ Model Answer: 1.Arc from vertex. 2.Equal arcs from each crossing. 3.Line from vertex through intersection.
Q3 Describe steps to construct triangle ABC: AB=5cm, BC=7cm, AC=6cm. (SSS) 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Draw the base, then swing arcs of the correct length from each endpoint.
✅ Model Answer: 1.Draw BC=7cm. 2.Compass 5cm, arc from B. 3.Compass 6cm, arc from C. 4.Intersection=A.
Q4 Describe constructing triangle: AB=8cm, AC=6cm, ∠BAC=50°. (SAS) 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Draw one side, use a protractor for the angle, mark the second side along that ray.
✅ Model Answer: 1.Draw AB=8cm. 2.Measure 50° at A. 3.Mark AC=6cm. 4.Connect BC.
Q5 Explain how to construct the circumscribed circle of a triangle. 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 The circumcentre is equidistant from all three vertices.
✅ Model Answer: 1.Perp bisector of two sides. 2.Intersection=circumcentre. 3.Radius=distance to vertex. 4.Draw circle.
Q6 Explain how to construct the inscribed circle of a triangle. 5 marks
Your Answer
💡 The incentre is equidistant from all three sides.
✅ Model Answer: 1.Bisect all three angles. 2.Intersection=incentre. 3.Perp from incentre to any side=radius. 4.Draw circle.
