Day 21 of 45 · Geometry

Geometry

Area & Perimeter of 2D Shapes
Foundation→Ordinary 6 questions · 21 marks total
📐
📘 What is this topic?

PERIMETER=distance around outside (linear units: cm, m). AREA=space covered (square units: cm², m²).

Sector: fraction of full circle. Sector area=(θ/360)×πr². Arc length=(θ/360)×2πr. Perimeter of sector=arc+2 radii.

All formulae are in the Tables booklet — know WHICH to choose and HOW to use it.

💡 Analogy

Area is how much paint you need to cover a surface. Perimeter is how much frame goes around the edge. 'How much carpet?'=area. 'How much fencing?'=perimeter.

🔍 Worked Example
Sector: r=9cm, angle=80°. Find area, arc and perimeter.
  1. Fraction: 80/360=2/9
  2. Area: (2/9)×81π=18π≈56.55 cm²
  3. Arc: (2/9)×18π=4π≈12.57 cm
  4. Perimeter: arc+2r=4π+18≈30.57 cm
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • Triangle area=½×base×HEIGHT (perpendicular height, not slant!)
  • Always include units: cm² for area, cm for perimeter
  • Sector perimeter=arc+TWO radii
📌 Key Formulae
Triangle: A=½bhCircle: A=πr²; C=2πrTrapezium: A=½(a+b)hSector: A=(θ/360)πr²; Arc=(θ/360)2πr
🎯 SEC Exam Insight: Area and sectors: sector perimeter = arc + TWO radii (students often forget the two straight sides). All formulae are in the Tables booklet — use it for every calculation.

Q1 Find the area of a triangle with base 10cm and height 6cm. 2 marks
Your Answer
💡 A=½×base×height (perpendicular height).
✅ Model Answer: A=½×10×6=30 cm²
Q2 Find area and circumference of a circle radius 7cm. 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 A=πr² and C=2πr with r=7.
✅ Model Answer: A=49π≈153.94 cm²; C=14π≈43.98 cm
Q3 Trapezium: parallel sides 5cm and 9cm, height 4cm. Find area. 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 A=½(a+b)×h where a and b are the parallel sides.
✅ Model Answer: A=½(5+9)×4=28 cm²
Q4 Sector: radius 8cm, angle 120°. Find area and arc length. 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Calculate the fraction of the full circle first (120/360=1/3).
✅ Model Answer: Area=(120/360)×64π=64π/3≈67.02 cm²; Arc=(120/360)×16π≈16.76 cm
Q5 Rectangle 12cm×10cm with circle radius 4cm removed. Find shaded area. 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Shaded area = total area − area of removed circle.
✅ Model Answer: Rectangle=120; Circle=16π≈50.27; Shaded≈69.73 cm²
Q6 Sector: radius 10cm, angle 80°. Find the perimeter of the sector. 5 marks
Your Answer
💡 Perimeter of sector = arc length + 2 radii.
✅ Model Answer: Arc=(80/360)×20π=40π/9≈13.96; Perimeter=13.96+20≈33.96 cm
