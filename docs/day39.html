Q1 Calculate: 3 + 4² × 2 − 1 2 marks
Your Answer
💡 BEMDAS: Exponent first, then multiply, then add/subtract.
✅ Model Answer: 4²=16; 16×2=32; 3+32−1=34
Q2 Find HCF and LCM of 24 and 36. 2 marks
Your Answer
💡 24=2³×3; 36=2²×3²
✅ Model Answer: HCF=12; LCM=72
Q3 Calculate: 3/8 + 5/6 2 marks
Your Answer
💡 Find the LCD (24), convert both fractions, then add.
✅ Model Answer: LCD=24; 9/24+20/24=29/24=1⁵⁄₂₄
Q4 €240 reduced by 15%. Find new price. 2 marks
Your Answer
💡 Multiplier for 15% decrease is (1−0.15)=0.85.
✅ Model Answer: 240×0.85=€204
Q5 Solve: 5x − 3 = 2x + 12 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 Collect x-terms on one side, numbers on the other.
✅ Model Answer: 3x=15; x=5
Q6 Expand: (x + 4)(x − 3) 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 Use FOIL: First, Outer, Inner, Last.
✅ Model Answer: x²+x−12
Q7 Solve: x² − x − 12 = 0 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 Find two numbers: product=−12, sum=−1.
✅ Model Answer: (x−4)(x+3)=0; x=4 or x=−3
Q8 Sequence 3,7,11,15,… Find T₁₀ and Tₙ. 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 a=3, d=4. Use Tₙ=a+(n−1)d.
✅ Model Answer: d=4; Tₙ=4n−1; T₁₀=39
Q9 Solve simultaneously: 3x+y=10 and x−2y=−1 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Substitution: rearrange equation 2 to get x=… then substitute.
✅ Model Answer: x=2y−1; 6y−3+y=10; y=13/7; x=19/7
Q10 Write as single fraction: 2/x + 3/(x+1) 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 LCD=x(x+1). Convert each fraction, then add numerators.
✅ Model Answer: [2(x+1)+3x]/[x(x+1)]=(5x+2)/(x²+x)
