Geometry

Mixed Problem Solving
Mixed 6 questions · 30 marks total
📐
📘 What is this topic?

Multi-step geometry: SEQUENCE different skills. Write each formula and sub-result clearly on its own line. Wrong early value? Examiners still give method marks for correct process in later steps.

💡 Analogy — How to picture it

A relay race: each runner (step) carries the baton (answer) to the next. Even if one runner makes an error, the next can still complete their section — examiners follow YOUR numbers through subsequent steps.

🔍 Worked Example
Isosceles triangle: base 16cm, equal sides 10cm. Area and perimeter.
  1. Drop perpendicular from apex — bisects base: 8cm + 8cm
  2. Pythagoras: h²=10²−8²=36 → h=6cm
  3. Area=½×16×6=48 cm²
  4. Perimeter=16+10+10=36 cm
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • Composite solids: calculate each part separately, then ADD
  • Angle of elevation: measured from the HORIZONTAL
  • When asked 'hence', use your PREVIOUS answer
📌 Key Formulae
Multi-step: identify→formula→substitute→evaluateComposite: V_total=V₁+V₂Consequential marks apply if you carry forward wrong values
🎯 SEC Exam Insight: Multi-step geometry: always show sub-answers clearly labelled. If an early step is wrong, consequential marks reward correct use of your wrong value in all subsequent steps.
Q1 Isosceles triangle: base 12cm, equal sides 10cm. Find area and perimeter. 5 marks
💡 Drop a perpendicular from the apex to find the height first.
✅ Answer: h=√(100−36)=8cm. Area=48 cm². Perimeter=32 cm
Q2 Swimming pool: trapezoidal cross-section (widths 6m and 10m, depth 4m), length 20m. Find volume. 5 marks
💡 V = area of cross-section × length.
✅ Answer: Cross-section=½(6+10)×4=32 m². V=32×20=640 m³
Q3 A(1,2), B(5,8). Find: (i)slope, (ii)|AB|, (iii)midpoint, (iv)equation of perpendicular bisector. 5 marks
💡 Find each in order — the perpendicular bisector goes through the midpoint with perpendicular slope.
✅ Answer: (i)3/2. (ii)2√13. (iii)(3,5). (iv)2x+3y=21
Q4 Wire 50m makes 32° with ground. Find mast height. From 60m from base, find angle of elevation. 5 marks
💡 Use sinθ=O/H for the first part, then tanθ=O/A for the second.
✅ Answer: h=50sin32°≈26.5m. tanα=26.5/60→α≈23.8°
Q5 Equilateral triangle, area 36√3 cm². Find side length, perimeter and inscribed circle radius. 5 marks
💡 Area of equilateral triangle=√3/4×s². Then find r=s/(2√3).
✅ Answer: √3/4×s²=36√3→s=12cm. P=36cm. r=2√3≈3.46cm
Q6 Cone: height=3×radius, volume=1000π cm³. Find r, h, slant height, lateral SA. 5 marks
💡 Substitute h=3r into V=⅓πr²h and solve for r.
✅ Answer: πr³=1000π→r=10cm; h=30cm; l=10√10≈31.6cm; LSA≈994 cm²
