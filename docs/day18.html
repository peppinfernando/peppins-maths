Day 18 of 45 · Geometry

Geometry

Lines, Angles & Triangles
Foundation 6 questions · 18 marks total
📐
📘 What is this topic?

Straight line=180°. Full turn=360°. Vertically opposite angles are EQUAL.

Triangle angles sum 180°. Exterior angle=sum of TWO opposite interior angles. Types: equilateral (3×60°), isosceles (2 equal sides, 2 equal angles), scalene (all different).

Parallel lines: alternate angles (Z)=equal; corresponding angles (F)=equal; co-interior angles (C)=180°.

💡 Analogy — How to picture it

Angles on a straight line summing to 180° is like sharing a pizza cut on a straight line — all slices on one side must together make a half-circle.

🔍 Worked Example
Parallel lines, transversal: (3x+10)° and (5x−30)°. Find x.
  1. Alternate angles (Z): must be equal
  2. 3x+10=5x−30
  3. 40=2x → x=20
  4. Angle=70°
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • Exterior angle=sum of OPPOSITE interior angles, NOT adjacent ones
  • Vertically opposite=EQUAL, not supplementary
  • Isosceles: only BASE ANGLES are equal
📌 Key Formulae
Triangle: A+B+C=180°Exterior angle=sum of 2 opposite interior anglesParallel lines: Z-angles equal; C-angles=180°
🎯 SEC Exam Insight: Angle theorems: always NAME the theorem before applying it. 'By the Exterior Angle Theorem…' — naming earns marks in proofs even if a calculation step has errors.
Q1 A triangle has angles 55°, 70° and x°. Find x. 2 marks
💡 Angles in a triangle sum to 180°.
✅ Answer: x=180−55−70=55°
Q2 Is the triangle from Q1 equilateral, isosceles or scalene? 2 marks
💡 Two equal angles means two equal sides.
✅ Answer: Isosceles: two angles are equal (55°), so two sides are equal.
Q3 A triangle has interior angles 45° and 80°. Find the exterior angle at the third vertex. 3 marks
💡 Exterior angle = sum of the TWO non-adjacent interior angles.
✅ Answer: Exterior angle=45+80=125°
Q4 Two straight lines cross. Vertically opposite angles are (2x+10)° and (3x−20)°. Find x. 3 marks
💡 Vertically opposite angles are equal — set them equal and solve.
✅ Answer: 2x+10=3x−20 → x=30°
Q5 Three angles on a straight line: (4y+10)°, (2y+5)° and 3y°. Find y. 4 marks
💡 Angles on a straight line sum to 180°.
✅ Answer: 9y+15=180 → y=165/9≈18.3°
Q6 A quadrilateral has angles 90°, 85°, 110° and x°. Find x. 4 marks
💡 Sum of angles in any quadrilateral = 360°.
✅ Answer: x=360−90−85−110=75°
