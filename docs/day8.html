Day 8 of 45 · Algebra

Algebra

Simplifying Expressions & Substitution
Foundation 6 questions · 18 marks total
✖️
📘 What is this topic?

LIKE TERMS have exactly the same variable and power. You can ONLY add/subtract like terms. 3x+5x=8x but 3x+5y cannot be combined.

SUBSTITUTE: replace every variable with the given number value. Always use BRACKETS around negative numbers to avoid sign errors.

💡 Analogy

Think of 'x' as a mystery box. 3x+2x = '3 boxes + 2 boxes = 5 boxes'. But 3x+2y is '3 boxes + 2 bags' — different objects, cannot be combined.

🔍 Worked Example
Evaluate 3a² − 2ab + b when a=2, b=−3
  1. Substitute: 3(2)²−2(2)(−3)+(−3)
  2. Exponents: 3(4)−2(2)(−3)+(−3)
  3. Multiply: 12−(−12)+(−3)
  4. Signs: 12+12−3
  5. Answer: 21
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • 3a+5b−2a = a+5b: collect a-terms, leave 5b alone
  • Substituting negative: x=−3 means (−3)²=9, NOT −3²=−9
  • 2x² means 2×(x²), NOT (2x)²=4x²
📌 Key Formulae
Like terms: same variable and powerSimplify: collect like termsSubstitute: replace each variable, then BEMDAS
🎯 SEC Exam Insight: Algebra substitution: put negative values in BRACKETS every time. The examiner awards a method mark for your substitution step, even before any arithmetic is done.

Q1 Simplify: 3a + 5b − 2a + b 2 marks
Your Answer
💡 Group the a-terms and the b-terms separately.
✅ Model Answer: a + 6b
Q2 Simplify: 5x² − 3x + x² + 4x 2 marks
Your Answer
💡 Collect x² terms together and x terms together.
✅ Model Answer: 6x² + x
Q3 Evaluate 2x² − 3x + 4 when x = 3. 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 Replace every x with 3. Use brackets and BEMDAS.
✅ Model Answer: 2(9)−3(3)+4 = 18−9+4 = 13
Q4 Find 3a² + 2ab − b² when a=2, b=−3. 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 Put negative values in brackets when substituting.
✅ Model Answer: 3(4)+2(2)(−3)−(9) = 12−12−9 = −9
Q5 Simplify: 3(2x − 1) − 2(x + 4) 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Expand each bracket first, being careful with the minus sign before the second bracket.
✅ Model Answer: 6x−3−2x−8 = 4x − 11
Q6 P = 2(l+w). If l=3x+1 and w=x−2, express P simplified in terms of x. 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Substitute expressions for l and w, simplify inside brackets, then multiply by 2.
✅ Model Answer: P=2(3x+1+x−2)=2(4x−1)=8x−2
