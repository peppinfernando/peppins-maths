Algebra Review

Exam-Style Mixed Algebra
Mixed 6 questions · 30 marks total
✖️
📘 What is this topic?

SEC exam presents algebra as multi-part questions (a)(b)(c) building progressively. 'Hence' means USE your previous answer — faster and shows mathematical connections.

Read ALL parts before starting (a). Later parts reveal what method part (a) should use.

💡 Analogy

Multi-part questions are like building a bridge: (a) lays the foundation; (b) builds the span; (c) crosses the bridge. Each part makes the next one possible.

🔍 Worked Example
Strategy: (a) Expand (x−3)(x+5). (b) Hence solve x²+2x−15=0.
  1. (a) Expand: x²+5x−3x−15=x²+2x−15
  2. (b) HENCE: x²+2x−15=0 → (x−3)(x+5)=0
  3. x=3 or x=−5
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • Never skip part (a) — later parts say 'hence' and need it
  • 'Hence or otherwise': use 'hence' route, it's faster
  • Always show formula/method name at each part start

Q2 f(x)=3x²−2x+1. (a) Find f(2). (b) Solve f(x)=10 (to 1 d.p.). (c) Find the minimum value of f(x). 5 marks
Your Answer
💡 For (b) set 3x²−2x+1=10, rearrange to =0, use quadratic formula.
✅ Model Answer: (a)9. (b)x≈2.0 or −1.3. (c)min at x=1/3; f(1/3)=2/3
Q3 Sequence 5,8,11,14,… (a) Find Tₙ. (b) Find T₂₀. (c) Is 200 in the sequence? 5 marks
Your Answer
💡 For (c) set Tₙ=200 and solve for n. Check if n is a whole number.
✅ Model Answer: (a)Tₙ=3n+2. (b)T₂₀=62. (c)3n+2=200→n=66. Yes, 200 is 66th term.
Q4 3 adults + 2 children = €40. 2 adults + 4 children = €38. Find adult and child prices. 5 marks
Your Answer
💡 Write two equations, then use elimination.
✅ Model Answer: 3a+2c=40; 2a+4c=38. (i)×2: 6a+4c=80. Subtract: 4a=42→a=€10.50; c=€4.25
Q5 S=ut+½at². Object falls from rest (u=0), a=10 m/s². How long to fall 150m? 5 marks
Your Answer
💡 Substitute u=0, a=10, S=150 and solve for t.
✅ Model Answer: 150=5t²→t²=30→t=√30≈5.48 seconds
