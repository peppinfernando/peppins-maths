Day 7 of 45 · Number

Applied Arithmetic

Ratio, Proportion, Currency & Tax
Ordinary 6 questions · 19 marks total
🔢
📘 What is this topic?

RATIO: to divide in a ratio, find total parts, calculate one part, multiply. DIRECT proportion: both quantities scale together. INVERSE proportion: one increases as the other decreases.

CURRENCY: multiply by exchange rate (€→foreign). IRISH TAX: 20% on first €36,800; 40% on rest. Subtract tax credits AFTER calculating gross tax.

💡 Analogy

Ratio is like a recipe. Flour:oats=3:5 for 4 people. For 24 people (6× bigger), you need 18 cups flour and 30 cups oats. The ratio stays fixed; the amounts scale.

🔍 Worked Example
Divide €560 in the ratio 3:4:7
  1. Total parts: 3+4+7 = 14
  2. One part: €560÷14 = €40
  3. First: 3×€40 = €120
  4. Second: 4×€40 = €160
  5. Third: 7×€40 = €280
  6. Check: 120+160+280=560 ✓
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • Always ADD parts before dividing the total
  • Inverse proportion: MORE workers=FEWER days. Use 1 worker first.
  • Tax credits reduce the TAX calculated, not the gross income
📌 Key Formulae
Ratio: 1 part=total÷sum of partsDirect: a₁/b₁=a₂/b₂Tax payable=gross tax−tax credits
🎯 SEC Exam Insight: Ratio and tax questions: always calculate each income tax band separately; subtract credits AFTER calculating gross tax. The multiplier method (×1.12 for VAT) is fastest.

Q1 Divide €400 in the ratio 3:5. 2 marks
Your Answer
💡 Add the ratio parts first: 3+5=8. Then find one part: €400÷8.
✅ Model Answer: 8 parts total; 1 part=€50; A=€150, B=€250
Q2 4 workers complete a job in 6 days. How long would 8 workers take? (Inverse proportion) 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 More workers = fewer days. Find how long for 1 worker first.
✅ Model Answer: 1 worker: 4×6=24 days. 8 workers: 24÷8=3 days
Q3 Convert €500 to dollars at €1=$1.12. 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 Multiply by the exchange rate to convert FROM euro.
✅ Model Answer: €500 × 1.12 = $560
Q4 Gross salary €42,000. Tax: 20% on first €36,800; 40% on balance. Tax credits €3,550. Find tax payable. 3 marks
Your Answer
💡 Calculate each tax band separately, add them, then subtract credits.
✅ Model Answer: Tax@20%=€7,360. Tax@40% on €5,200=€2,080. Gross tax=€9,440. Less credits: €5,890
Q5 Laptop costs €120 before VAT at 23%. Find the total price. 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 Multiply by the VAT rate to find the VAT amount, then add to original price.
✅ Model Answer: VAT=€120×0.23=€27.60. Total=€147.60
Q6 Cement:sand:gravel = 1:2:4. 840 kg needed. How much of each? 4 marks
Your Answer
💡 7 total parts. Find 1 part first, then multiply for each ingredient.
✅ Model Answer: 7 parts; 1 part=120 kg; Cement=120, Sand=240, Gravel=480 kg
